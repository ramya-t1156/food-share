About FoodShare

We at FoodShare aim to combat food waste and hunger by redistributing excess food to those in need.

Our Vision

FoodShare aims to reduce food waste and address food insecurity through technology and community collaboration. It connects surplus food providers with nearby recipients in need, diverting excess food from landfills. By fostering community sharing, FoodShare promotes the redistribution of surplus food resources among neighbors.

Features like donation tracking ensure transparency and accountability in the redistribution process. The website encourages users to take collective responsibility for reducing food waste and supporting those in need. Through its platform, FoodShare promotes a more sustainable and equitable food system. Its objective is to leverage technology to create a positive impact on food sustainability and accessibility.

Our Approach

Transparency and Accountability: Central to FoodShare's approach is ensuring transparency and accountability in the redistribution process. It incorporates features like donation tracking to provide users with visibility into how surplus food resources are allocated. This emphasis on transparency encourages collective responsibility among users for reducing food waste and supporting those in need.

Leveraging Technology for Redistribution: FoodShare's mobile app connects surplus food providers with nearby recipients, efficiently redistributing resources

About Us

The basic concept of this project Food Waste Management is to collect the excess/leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls, etc. and distribute to the needy people.
